About the Artist

Welcome to Dream Canvas, a small studio based in Hemel Hempstead, UK. I'm a self‑taught artist who fell in love with painting as a way of expressing emotion through colour and form. Over the years I’ve developed a style that blends abstract expressionism with contemporary design – sweeping brushstrokes, bold palettes and geometry that hint at landscapes, galaxies and cityscapes. Each painting you see in the shop is a one‑of‑a‑kind original created by me.

My goal with this website is to create a space that reflects the spirit of the artwork and makes it easy for you to explore the collection. I believe art should be accessible and personal; if a piece speaks to you, I hope it will bring joy and inspiration into your home.

When I’m not in the studio you’ll find me exploring the Chilterns, gathering inspiration from nature’s ever‑changing light and patterns. Thank you for visiting and supporting independent artists!
